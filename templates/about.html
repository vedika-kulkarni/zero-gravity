About Us

Nothing Holds Us Down

Zero Gravity is a non-profit organisation based in Nagpur, Maharashtra under the Dr. Shrikant Jichkar Foundation.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged youth by providing access to education, health, and community support — breaking barriers that hold them back from achieving their fullest potential.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of background, has equal opportunity to thrive. We envision Nagpur as a model city for inclusive community development.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and resilience drive everything we do. We believe in sustainable impact — building communities that lift themselves up through unity and purpose.
